Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

◎

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect only the information necessary to provide the service. This includes:

  • Account information — your name and email address provided at registration.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and notes you enter manually.
  • Usage data — basic logs such as login timestamps, used to maintain account security.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data from your device beyond what you explicitly enter.

◈

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate and improve Spendly. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Display your expenses and summaries within your personal dashboard.
  • Authenticate your identity when you log in.
  • Diagnose technical issues and improve the reliability of the service.

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

§

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in a database accessible only to you and the application. We take reasonable technical precautions to protect your data, including:

  • Passwords are stored as secure hashes — never in plain text.
  • Database access is restricted and not publicly exposed.
  • Sessions are managed securely to prevent unauthorized access.

While we take data security seriously, no system is completely immune to risk. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

⊘

4. Third Party Services

Spendly uses a small number of third-party services to deliver the experience, including:

  • Google Fonts — to load typography. Google may log the font request but receives no personal data from your account.
  • No analytics platforms, advertising networks, or tracking pixels are used.

We do not integrate with any financial institutions, payment processors, or external APIs that access your bank or card data.

↻

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding your privacy or this policy — including requests to delete your account and data — please reach out to us directly.

  • Email: hello@spendly.app
  • We aim to respond to all privacy-related requests within 5 business days.

This policy may be updated occasionally. Continued use of Spendly after changes are posted means you accept the revised policy.
